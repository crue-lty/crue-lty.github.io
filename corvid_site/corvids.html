Corvid species found in Poland

  • Common raven (Corvus corax)
  • Eurasian jay (Garrulus glandarius)
  • Eurasian magpie (Pica pica)
  • Hooded crow (Corvus cornix)
  • Rook (Corvus frugilegus)
  • Spotted nutcracker (Nucifraga caryocatactes)
  • Western jackdaw (Coloeus monedula)

  2. Eurasian jay: 34-35 cm; 52-58 cm
  3. Eurasian magpie: 44-46 cm; 52-62 cm
  4. Hooded crow: 48-52 cm; 105 cm
  5. Rook: 44-46 cm; 81-99 cm
  6. Spotted nutcracker: 32-38 cm; 49-53 cm
  7. Western jackdaw: 34-39 cm; 67-74 cm
