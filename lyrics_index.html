Song lyric index

A while ago my friend asked me for help with transcribing some song lyrics. I figured that I could write them down somewhere, sort them and have them sit around neatly. So this website happened :3

Here it is - my project. I want to try and write down the lyrics to (some, probably not all) songs of some old obscure music projects.
Although, honestly, I might end up asking the authors for help once I get too stuck and won't be able to progress anymore. Listening to old cassette recordings is certainly not the easiest!
So, I'm hoping that I can get that figured out. I might write down some means of contact to myself here if you happen to stumble upon this site and want to give the songs a listen, see if you can hear something better than I can.
